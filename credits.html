About Worlds Within Worlds

"Worlds Within Worlds" is a collaborative project by Mithila Fox and Ed Hebert.

Our project consists of six separate vignettes, each offering a different visual interpretation of a literary passage or inspirational quote. Combined into a cohesive presentation, it explores experimental distortions of scale, perspective, and time.
